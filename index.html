Just Enough Acres

A hobby farm located in Duvall, WA

"No Goats, No Glory"
Est. 2015

Welcome to Just Enough Acres, a family owned hobby farm located just outside the town of Duvall, Washington. We're just getting started, but in just over a year we've gone from one Chihuahua to 23 animals, including 8 goats, 7 ducks, 4 chickens, 2 cats, and 2 dogs. We've also added a small greenhouse, a raspberry patch, and 320 square feet of raised garden beds. This year we should have 2-4 baby goats, which means 2 of our goats will be producing milk, so we should be able to make some cheese! We're also expecting eggs from 5 of our ducks and all 4 of our chickens. There are 2 male ducks in with the females, so hopefully we'll get some ducklings as well.
